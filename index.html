About the Event

The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is held at the beautiful Brooklyn Bridge Park from August 5th through 8th of August 2019, from 6pm until midnight. Organized by Jennifer Viala, the founder of Brooklyn Vibes Events Co., with the intention of promoting a great outdoor movie experience for film lovers of all ages, The Event its free of charge, but pre-registering is necessary in order to secure a spot. What are you waiting for? Scroll down the pega and pre-register to join us in this amazing experience!

Harry Sanborn (Jack Nicholson) is a perennial playboy with a libido much younger than his years.
